About This Tracker

The Cerulean Labs Space Debris Risk Tracker visualizes all tracked in-orbit debris from the US Space Surveillance Network (18th SDS), sourced via Space-Track.org.

Risk Scoring Methodology (4 factors, 0-10 scale)

Altitude (0-3): Perigee 600-1000km = 3 (critical density zone), 400-600 or 1000-1500km = 2, other = 1
Size (0-3): LARGE = 3, MEDIUM = 2, SMALL = 1 (based on radar cross-section)
Inclination (0-2): 95-105° (sun-synchronous) = 2, 60-85° = 1, other = 0
Longevity (0-2): Perigee ≥800km = 2, ≥600km = 1, other = 0

Total → CRITICAL (≥8), HIGH (≥6), MODERATE (≥4), LOW (≥1)

To update data: download a SATCAT debris CSV from Space-Track.org, then click "Update Data" in the header.
